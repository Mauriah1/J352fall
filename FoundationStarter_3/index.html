Navient Complaints

Mauriah Allen / November 13, 2023 / Navient Data visualization

Navinet Corporation is one of the nation's largest federal student loan services. Naviant has been experiencing a growing number of lawsuits. This includes lawsuits from the Consumber Finanical Protection Bureu, state attorneys, and a group of teachers. Navient has been required by 39 state attorney generals to provide a total of $1.85 billion in relief for private and federal student loan borrowers. Concluding the settlement, Navient has had a increasment of lawsuits in other sections including debt collection and credit reporting. More than 66,000 Navient private student loan borrowers who were taken advantage of will receive complete loan cancellation of their remaining balances. However, naviant still has some work to do.
